Sports have always been a huge part of my life for as long as I can remember. I have played many different sports ever since I was young. My favorites that I’ve played are basketball,softball, and volleyball.

          Basketball is my absolute favorite sport. I have been playing for 8 years. My favorite basketball memory would have to be just spending time with my teammates. I love           them all so much, and no matter win or loss, we always have a good time.

          I haven’t been playing softball for very long, but I have grown to love it quickly. It is hard since I have only been playing for one year. I love softball season so much           because it’s warm out, school’s almost over, and everything is perfect.

          I have a love-hate relationship with volleyball. I love it most of the time, but other times I get frustrated quickly. I like doing my hair before games, and just being with my           team. My favorite memory would have to be cheering on the bench. One time the opposing student section called me out and told me to sit down.
